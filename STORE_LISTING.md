## Full description (App Store & Play Store)

Vitalis is a calm, beautiful habit tracker built to help you show up for
yourself every day — without the guilt-trips or clutter.

**Build habits that last**
Add daily, weekly, or monthly habits in seconds. Check in with a single tap and
watch your streak grow. A GitHub-style heatmap shows every check-in at a glance,
so your progress is impossible to ignore.

**See your wellness at a glance**
Your Health Hub turns the last 7 days into a single Wellness Score, so you always
know how you're trending — no spreadsheets required.

**Private AI wellness coach**
Get short, encouraging, personalized insights based on your real habits and
goals. Your data is analyzed securely on the server — your habits never leave
your account, and there are no ads and no data selling.

**Gentle reminders**
Set a reminder time per habit and Vitalis will nudge you at the right moment.
Turn them all off anytime.

**Yours, and private**
- Secure sign-in with email or Google
- Row-level security: only you can ever read your data
- Dark mode
- Export your full history as JSON whenever you want
- Full accessibility support (VoiceOver & TalkBack)

Vitalis offers general wellness information only and is not a medical device or a
substitute for professional medical advice.

Start small. Stay consistent. Feel the difference. 🌿
