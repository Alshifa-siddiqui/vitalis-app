Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 June 2026

Vitalis ("we", "us", the "app") is a personal wellness and habit-tracking app. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your rights. We designed Vitalis to collect as little as possible.

Who we are

Vitalis is developed by Alshifa Siddiqui. Contact: siddiqui21shifa@gmail.com.

What we collect

  • Account data — your email address and an encrypted password (handled by our authentication provider, Supabase). If you sign in with Google, we receive your email and basic profile from Google.
  • App data you create — your habits, check-in history, streaks, display name, and any optional health-profile values you enter (age, weight, height, goals, notes). Health-profile fields are optional and stored only if you choose to enter them.
  • AI feature input — when you tap "Get AI Insight," a summary of your habits and goals is sent to our secure server function, which forwards it to Anthropic's Claude API to generate wellness text. We do not send your name, email, or raw health-report files.
  • Technical data — standard request metadata (e.g. timestamps) needed to operate the service.

We do not sell your data, show ads, or use third-party advertising trackers.

How we use it

  • To provide the app: authenticate you, store and sync your habits, and generate insights you request.
  • To keep the service secure and working.

Where it's stored & who can see it

  • Your data is stored in our Supabase (PostgreSQL) database, protected by Row-Level Security — meaning only your authenticated account can read or write your rows.
  • Authentication tokens are stored encrypted on your device (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore).
  • Sub-processors: Supabase (database, auth, hosting) and Anthropic (AI insight generation). Each processes data only to provide their service.

Health data

Vitalis provides general wellness information only — not medical advice, and is not a medical device. Health-profile values you enter are treated as sensitive and protected the same way as the rest of your data.

Your rights

  • Access / export — you can export your data from within the app (Profile → Privacy & data).
  • Delete — you can delete all your habit data in-app, and request full account deletion (Profile → Privacy & data, or email us). We will delete your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent — stop using the app and delete your account at any time.

Depending on your region (e.g. EU/UK GDPR, California CCPA), you may have additional rights to access, correct, or erase your data; contact us to exercise them.

Data retention

We keep your data while your account is active. When you delete your account, we remove your personal data from our active systems.

Children

Vitalis is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date will change. Material changes will be surfaced in the app or by email.

Contact

Questions or requests: siddiqui21shifa@gmail.com.

Vitalis offers general wellness information only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice.
